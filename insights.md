# Key Insights

This dataset contains 3,500 e-commerce sales records between 2022 and 2024.

## Overall Summary

- Total Sales: $10,667,881.00
- Total Profit: $1,844,665.21
- Total Quantity Sold: 17,261 units

## Main Findings

### Electronics performed the best overall
Electronics generated the highest sales and profit out of all categories, with:

- Sales: $5,326,074.00
- Profit: $923,185.59

### The West region led in both sales and profit
The West region had the strongest overall performance with:

- Sales: $2,844,450.00
- Profit: $495,358.73

### A few products drove a large portion of sales
The top-selling products were:

1. Camera
2. Monitor
3. Printer
4. Mouse
5. Smartphone

Most of the highest-performing products were electronics and accessories.

## Takeaway

The dataset suggests that electronics are the main driver of both revenue and profitability.
